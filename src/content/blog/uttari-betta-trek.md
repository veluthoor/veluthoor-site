<p>This will be a super short post, but wanted to make this an archive of all the treks I've done so here it goes.</p><p>Again did the night trek, so left Bangalore at 11pm the previous night and after 2 hours of getting stuck in Bangalore traffic, we reached the base point by around 3am. This time I went with 3 of my college friends. </p><p>I'd say this is less of a trek and more of a short hike. I believe it took us a little less than 30minutes to reach the peak. For 80% of the course, there were proper steps that were made, giving the place very pilgrimage like energy. It's best done as a night trek though, as it can get quite hot during the day as there is very little green cover on the way. Overall, the trek itself was nothing too enthralling. <br><br>However, at the summit, there is a cave that has some interesting obstacle course that truly makes this trek worth completing.
